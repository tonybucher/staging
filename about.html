About Me

How I see the world.

From early on, I have been entranced by "story." It wasn't until after college that I found my place in story-telling. Over the past 8 years, I have explored story telling from a variety of outlets. Whether it was photography, videography, logo identity, or as subtle as movement in text, I found a story in design. Story is everywhere, even if it's not immediately recognized.

I have worked with target audiences of every age: teenagers, high school parents, sandwich generations, and professionals. I have worked in a wide spectrum of industries including: religious, non-profits, finance, enterainment, and data. I get excited when I can take my knowledge of industry standards and audience patterns to create memorable moments in products.
